Privacy Policy

POPIA • GDPR • ISO 27001 Compliant — because privacy and trust go hand in hand.

1. Introduction

Welcome to All Sitters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and ISO 27001 information-security standards.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal identifiers such as your name, contact details, and address.
  • Authentication data for secure login and verification.
  • Police-clearance and identification documents (sitters only).
  • Payment and transaction data when using escrow or Paystack/crypto gateways.
  • Device and usage information for security and analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

We process your information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Verify sitter credentials and police clearance certificates.
  • Facilitate escrow-based payments and payouts.
  • Provide customer support and fraud prevention.
  • Improve services through secure, anonymised analytics.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal data only when there is a lawful basis, including consent, performance of a contract, legal obligation, or legitimate interest. Users may withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior lawful processing.

5. Data Storage & Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We host infrastructure on AWS with access controls, logging, and continuous compliance monitoring aligned with ISO 27001. Personal data is retained only as long as necessary for legal and operational requirements.

6. International Transfers

Some data may be stored or processed outside South Africa using secure, GDPR-compliant cloud services. Cross-border transfers occur only where equivalent protection measures are in place.

7. Your Rights

  • Access and obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction, deletion, or restriction of processing.
  • Object to certain types of processing or data use.
  • Withdraw consent and close your account at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (SA) or your local authority under GDPR.

8. Artificial Intelligence

All Sitters integrates AI technologies to enhance safety, recommendations, and automation. No AI model is used to make final employment or financial decisions without human oversight. Training data and analytics are anonymised to protect your identity.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal, privacy-respecting cookies for authentication and analytics. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

10. Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect legal, security, or operational changes. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the effective date shown below.

Effective date: 5 November 2025

11. Contact Us

For questions or data-access requests, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@allsitters.com.
